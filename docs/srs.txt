# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

FleetFlow is a web-based Fleet & Logistics Management System developed to replace traditional manual fleet management with a centralized digital platform.

The system helps organizations efficiently manage:

- Vehicles
- Drivers
- Trips
- Maintenance
- Fuel Expenses
- Financial Reports
- Fleet Analytics

The application improves operational efficiency, reduces manual work, prevents scheduling conflicts, and provides real-time visibility into fleet operations.

---

## 1.2 Scope

FleetFlow enables logistics companies to manage the complete lifecycle of fleet operations.

The system provides:

- Secure authentication
- Vehicle management
- Driver management
- Trip planning
- Maintenance tracking
- Fuel expense tracking
- Analytics dashboard
- Financial reporting

---

## 1.3 Objectives

The project aims to:

- Digitize fleet management
- Reduce operational errors
- Improve driver safety
- Prevent overloading
- Reduce maintenance costs
- Improve fleet utilization
- Generate business reports
- Support data-driven decisions

# 10. Database Planning (Entities)

Main entities:

- User
- Role
- Vehicle
- Driver
- Trip
- Trip Assignment
- Maintenance
- Fuel Log
- Expense
- Report

Key relationships:

- One **Role** can belong to many **Users**.
- One **Vehicle** can have many **Trips**, **Maintenance Records**, **Fuel Logs**, and **Expenses**.
- One **Driver** can be assigned to many **Trips** over time.
- Each **Trip** references one **Vehicle** and one **Driver**.
- **Expenses** may optionally be linked to a specific **Trip** for detailed cost tracking.
